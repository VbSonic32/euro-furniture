Our Story

Born from a
love of Europe

FurniEuro was founded in 2011 by Marcus and Elise Vander, a couple who spent years traveling through the ateliers and design studios of Milan, Copenhagen, Hamburg, and Paris — and fell in love with the craft behind every piece.

Returning to their home in Conroe, Texas, they found a market hungry for quality but underserved by what was available locally. So they built a bridge: a showroom where European design excellence meets Texas hospitality.

"We don't sell furniture. We help people build spaces that feel like home — their truest, most beautiful home."
Marcus & Elise Vander
Co-founders, FurniEuro
